# Hardware Monitoring Tools

These tools provide detailed information about your system's hardware components and monitor their status in real-time.

## Tools Overview

### 1. CPU-Z
- **File**: `CPU-Z.exe`
- **Function**: Provides comprehensive information about your processor (name, number, codename, process, package, cache levels), motherboard, and chipset.
- **Usage**: Use this to verify CPU clock speeds, RAM timings, and motherboard BIOS versions.

### 2. GPU-Z
- **File**: `GPU-Z.exe`
- **Function**: A lightweight utility designed to give you all information about your video card and GPU.
- **Usage**: Use this to check GPU clock speeds, memory type, driver version, and real-time sensor data (temperature, load).

### 3. HWMonitor
- **File**: `HW-Monitor.exe`
- **Function**: Hardware monitoring program that reads PC systems main health sensors: voltages, temperatures, fans speed.
- **Usage**: Crucial for monitoring system stability and ensuring components are not overheating during stress tests or gaming.

## Recommended Usage
It is highly recommended to check your hardware status before and after applying optimizations to ensure stability and monitor any changes in temperature or performance.
