Driver Booster is not very recommended as it often installs generic and unofficial drivers.

My recommendation is that you use DriverIdentifier since it directly analyzes your Hardware ID
and gives you the best drivers for it.

If you get low performance in games, it's probably because you don't have a video driver installed.
I recommend that you learn more about this by researching on the internet since it's not a difficult topic
and it can help you a lot!
